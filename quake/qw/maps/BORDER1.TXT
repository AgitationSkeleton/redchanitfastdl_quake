======================= Game Play ======================== 

This is a TeamFortress map.

There are 3 teams,
        1. Border patrol - they kill everyone
        2. Hispanic Immigrants - They try to take the truck and go to
           america.
        3. MiB, they help the Immigrants get to America (kill border patrol)

POINT SYSTEM:

        Every immigrant that makes it to america gets 5 personal frags, and
        everyone on yellow and red get one frag
